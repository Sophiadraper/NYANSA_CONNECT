Fun fact: Nyansa means 'wisdom through learning'

Who are we? NYANSA Connect is a community based non-profit working with rural schools in Ghana to deliver NYANSA Debate clubs. Since 2018 and with over 70 active members across rural Ghana, NYANSA Connect provides a platform for children to develop confident voices whilst increasing educational attainment.
